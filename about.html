Founder’s message

I founded Magic Math Master to help students build confidence, clarity, and strong foundations in mathematics — without stress.

I grew up with limited access to high-quality expert guidance, despite a strong love for mathematics. These early challenges shaped my belief that every motivated child deserves clear teaching and meaningful support.

My goal is to make high-quality maths education accessible through personalised teaching, small groups, and calm, structured learning pathways.

Warm regards,
Dr Tuyet Vu
Founder & Educator

Real-world applied mathematics

Alongside teaching, I work in Australian government research, developing mathematical models and tracking algorithms used in real-world applications such as satellite tracking and signal analysis.

This experience allows me to teach maths as a way of thinking — not just memorising formulas, but reasoning, modelling, and problem-solving.

Teaching background

I have taught mathematics across Vietnam and Australia, including secondary schools, universities, and teacher-training programs.

I have worked with students from Prep to Year 12, university students, and mentored over 100 trainee teachers and undergraduates.
