✨ Founder’s message

Welcome to Magic Math Master — where passion and precision come together to build success in mathematics.

I was raised in a family with limited means, yet I developed a deep love for mathematics and a strong habit of self-study. Although I worked hard, I did not always have access to high-quality expert guidance to help me reach my full potential. These early challenges shaped my purpose.

I founded Magic Math Master to create opportunities for children who are curious and capable, but may not have the resources or support to advance. My goal is to make high-quality mathematics education more accessible through personalised teaching, small-group learning, and flexible support options for families.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Parents can expect: clear goals, structured lessons, and regular updates about progress and next steps.

Warm regards,
Dr Tuyet Vu
Founder, Magic Math Master
