Programs

Select a pathway based on your child’s goals: catch up, keep up, move ahead, or build exam confidence. All programs are structured, calm, and aligned with Australian school learning.

Primary Mathematics (Prep–Year 6)

Build strong foundations and confidence through clear explanation and meaningful practice.

  • Number sense, arithmetic fluency, mental strategies
  • Fractions/decimals/percentages with visual understanding
  • Word-problem strategies (read–plan–solve–check)
  • NAPLAN preparation support (where relevant)

Secondary Mathematics (Years 7–10)

Strengthen algebraic thinking and problem-solving for school assessments and confidence in class.

  • Algebra, equations, indices, linear relationships
  • Geometry, measurement, trigonometry foundations
  • Reasoning and multi-step problem solving
  • Assessment preparation + revision planning

Exam Preparation & Extension

For students aiming higher results or needing a clear plan for tests, NAPLAN, and school exams.

  • Exam strategy: time management, checking methods, reducing careless mistakes
  • Targeted practice: focus on high-impact weaknesses
  • Extension challenges for advanced learners
  • Confidence building and growth mindset support
