📌 Important information (short summary)

This is a quick overview. For full details, see the sections below.

  • Payment: Fees are paid before lessons.
  • Cancellations: Please provide at least 24 hours’ notice.
  • Refunds: May be available with 24+ hours’ notice or a medical certificate (where applicable).
  • Late arrival: Lessons end at the scheduled time.
  • No-show: Lessons are charged.
  • Rescheduling: Usually up to once per booking (subject to availability).
  • Online lessons: Stable internet and a quiet space are required.
  • Behaviour: Respectful, supportive learning environment.
  • Practice: Regular practice helps students progress faster.
  • Communication: We aim to respond within 24–48 business hours.
  • Privacy: Student information is not shared without permission.

Policy details

Below are the key policies. If anything is unclear, please contact us — we’re happy to help.

💳 Payment policy

  • Payment is required before lessons begin.
  • Fees are billed monthly or via prepaid lesson blocks (as agreed).
  • Payment is typically via bank transfer.
  • Fees may change with reasonable notice.

🗓️ 2. Cancellations & changes

  • Please provide at least 24 hours’ notice for cancellations or changes.
  • Late cancellations may be charged (the slot is reserved for your child).
  • If your child is unwell, contact us as early as possible.

⏱️ 3. Late arrival & no-show

  • Lessons finish at the scheduled end time (even if arriving late).
  • No-shows are charged (the time was reserved).
  • If running late, please message us — we will do our best to help.

🔁 4. Rescheduling

  • Rescheduling is subject to availability.
  • Typically allowed up to once per booking.
  • Lesson packs may have an expiry date — please use sessions within the valid period.

💻 5. Online lessons

  • A stable internet connection and a quiet learning space are required.
  • Please be ready on time so we can use the full session effectively.
  • If technical issues occur, we will work with you to minimise disruption where possible.

🤝 6. Behaviour & wellbeing

  • We maintain a respectful, supportive learning environment.
  • Students are encouraged to ask questions and learn from mistakes.
  • Bullying, disrespect, or unsafe behaviour is not tolerated.

📚 7. Practice & progress

  • Regular practice is essential for steady improvement.
  • We may recommend short weekly tasks to strengthen skills.
  • Progress is fastest when lessons + practice are consistent.

🔒 8. Communication & privacy

  • We aim to respond within 24–48 business hours.
  • Student information is kept private and is not shared without permission.
  • Photos/videos are only used with consent (if applicable).

Questions about policies?

Send us your question — we’re happy to clarify and recommend the best next step.

Tip: Tell us your child’s year level + goal (catch up / keep up / get ahead) so we can reply faster.
